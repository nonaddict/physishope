How to Choose a Laptop for College

Make the right choice with this complete laptop buying guide for students.

Published: June 2025

1. Performance & Specs

Look for at least an Intel i5 laptop with 8GB RAM for most college work. If you study design or programming, consider Intel i7 models with 16GB RAM for better performance.

2. Battery Life

Choose a laptop that offers 8+ hours of battery life to last through long lectures without needing to recharge.

3. Portability

Lightweight models like 13-inch ultrabooks are ideal for students who move around campus frequently.

4. Budget

Set a realistic budget: excellent student laptops are available under $700 depending on your needs.

5. Bonus Tips

  • Look for student discounts from manufacturers.
  • Make sure to get good warranty coverage.
  • Consider extended storage (256GB SSD minimum).
